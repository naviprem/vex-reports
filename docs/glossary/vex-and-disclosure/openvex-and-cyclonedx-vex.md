# OpenVEX & CycloneDX VEX

The lighter alternative [[vex|VEX]] formats. **OpenVEX** is [[sigstore-cosign|Sigstore]]-native — attachable to images as cosign attestations, verified with the same tooling as image signatures. **CycloneDX VEX** embeds in CycloneDX [[sbom|SBOMs]].

Out of scope for this project's Phase 1 ([[csaf|CSAF]] only), but the internal canonical record is designed not to preclude emitting them later.
